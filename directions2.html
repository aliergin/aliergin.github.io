DIRECTIONS TO WINLAB

Directions from New Jersey Turnpike (North and South), Newark Airport and New York City:
 Take the New Jersey Turnpike to Exit 9. As you exit the Turnpike, take Route 18 North to Route 1 South. Take Route 1 South approximately 1.5 miles and follow the signs for the Technology Centre of New Jersey (just past Bristol-Myers Squibb). Turn right into the Technology Centre and follow the road around to the left. After the stop sign, the road then curves around to the right and the Chubbs building will be on your right. Continue until you can't go any further. Make a right and go to the next driveway on the left into the parking lot for Building 671 (Yellow Trim). Parking permits are NOT required for this parking area.

Directions from the Princeton Area (South of New Brunswick on Route 1):
Take Route 1 North. Once you pass the Milltown Road Exit watch for signs to the Technology Centre of New Jersey. It will appear as though you are entering the driveway for DeVry University. You will follow the road around to the left to the end, make a left and go under Route 1 to enter the Technology Centre parking areas. Follow signs for Rutgers Tech Center II. Go to Building 671 (Yellow Trim).  Parking permits are NOT required for this parking area.

Directions from I-287:
Take exit for Route 1 South. When you pass the entrance to Cook College, watch for signs for the Technology Centre of New Jersey (just past Bristol-Myers Squibb).  Turn right into the Technology Centre and follow the road around to the left. After the stop sign, the road then curves around to the right and the Chubbs building will be on your right. Continue until you can't go any further. Make a right and go to the next driveway on the left into the parking lot for Building 671 (Yellow Trim).  Parking permits are NOT required for this parking area.

Take Route 18 North to Route 1 South. Take Route 1 South approximately 1.5 miles and follow signs for the Technology Centre of New Jersey (just past Bristol-Myers Squibb).
